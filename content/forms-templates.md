## Using Forms and Templates

1. **Download** the form or template you need
2. **Fill out** the form completely with required information
3. **Submit** the completed form to the appropriate department via email
4. **Keep a copy** for your records

## Form Categories

Our forms are organized into categories for easy navigation:

- **Proposals**: Research proposal and project application forms
- **Ethics**: Ethics review and compliance forms
- **Lab Management**: Laboratory access and equipment request forms
- **Data**: Data access and management forms
- **Funding**: Grant application templates and funding forms
- **Collaboration**: Collaboration agreement templates
- **Publications**: Publication submission and documentation forms

## Submission Instructions

Completed forms should be submitted to:
- **Email**: surc@su.edu.krd
- **Subject**: Form Name - [Your Name]
- **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days for standard forms

For urgent requests, please contact the relevant department directly.
