## Laboratory Overview

The Biology Laboratory provides state-of-the-art facilities for molecular biology, microbiology, and cell biology research. The lab is equipped with modern instruments for DNA/RNA analysis, cell culture, and microscopy.

## Research Areas

- Molecular biology
- Genetics
- Cell biology
- Microbiology
- Biotechnology

## Facilities

- Biosafety cabinets for sterile work
- Cell culture facilities
- Cold storage for samples
- Microscopy suite
- Molecular biology workstations
