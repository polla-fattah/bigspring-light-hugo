## Laboratory Overview

The Chemistry Laboratory is a state-of-the-art facility equipped with modern analytical instruments to support cutting-edge research in chemistry and materials science. Our facility provides researchers with access to advanced spectroscopy, chromatography, and analytical equipment.

## Research Areas

- Analytical chemistry
- Materials science
- Organic synthesis
- Environmental chemistry
- Chemical analysis

## Facilities

- Fume hoods for safe chemical handling
- Analytical instrumentation
- Sample preparation areas
- Computer workstations for data analysis
- Storage facilities for chemicals and samples

## Access and Booking

For access to this laboratory or specific equipment, please contact the lab coordinator or use the booking system (coming soon).
