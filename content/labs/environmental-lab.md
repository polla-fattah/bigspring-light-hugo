## Laboratory Overview

The Environmental Research Laboratory provides specialized facilities and equipment for environmental sample analysis, including water quality testing, soil analysis, and air quality monitoring. The lab supports research in environmental science and sustainability.

## Research Areas

- Water quality analysis
- Soil science
- Air quality monitoring
- Environmental chemistry
- Climate change research

## Facilities

- Sample preparation areas
- Instrumentation room
- Cold storage for samples
- Field equipment storage
- Data analysis workstations

## Access and Booking

For access to equipment or field sampling equipment, please contact the lab coordinator.
