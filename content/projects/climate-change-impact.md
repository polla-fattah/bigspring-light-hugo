Comprehensive study on climate change impacts on Kurdistan's ecosystems and agricultural systems. This project investigates the effects of climate change on regional ecosystems and agricultural productivity.

## Project Overview

This research project examines the impacts of climate change on Kurdistan's ecosystems and agricultural systems, providing valuable insights for policy makers and farmers.

## Objectives

- Assess climate change impacts on ecosystems
- Evaluate effects on agricultural productivity
- Develop adaptation strategies
- Provide policy recommendations

## Methodology

- Data collection and analysis
- Field research
- Statistical modeling
- Impact assessment

## Related Publications

This project has produced several publications on climate change impacts and statistical modeling.

## Research Team

The project is led by researchers from the Environmental Studies Unit with collaboration from the Data Analysis Unit.
