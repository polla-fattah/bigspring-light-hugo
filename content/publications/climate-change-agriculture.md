This publication provides a comprehensive analysis of climate change effects on agricultural productivity in the Kurdistan Region. The research examines temperature patterns, precipitation changes, and their impacts on crop yields and farming practices.

## Key Findings

- Detailed analysis of climate patterns over the past decade
- Impact assessment on major crops in the region
- Recommendations for sustainable agricultural adaptation
