SURC operates under strict ethical standards and follows comprehensive guidelines to ensure research integrity, ethical conduct, and compliance with university policies and international standards.

## Research Ethics

All research conducted under SURC must adhere to the highest ethical standards. Our ethics guidelines cover:

- Informed consent procedures
- Protection of human subjects
- Animal research ethics
- Data privacy and confidentiality
- Conflict of interest management
- Ethical review processes

## Data Management

Research data management is crucial for research integrity and reproducibility. Our policies ensure:

- Proper data documentation and metadata
- Secure data storage and backup procedures
- Data sharing and open access principles
- Data retention and archiving policies
- Compliance with data protection regulations

## Compliance and Review

All research projects must undergo appropriate ethical review and comply with:

- University research policies
- National research regulations
- International research standards
- Funding agency requirements
- Institutional review board guidelines
