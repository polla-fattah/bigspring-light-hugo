## Data Access Levels

- **Open Access**: Publicly available datasets that can be downloaded directly
- **Restricted Access**: Requires registration and agreement to data use terms
- **On-Request**: Available upon request, subject to approval and data sharing agreements

## Data Management Guidelines

SURC follows international standards for research data management. All researchers are encouraged to:

- Document data collection methods and procedures
- Use consistent naming conventions and file structures
- Maintain data backups and version control
- Follow ethical guidelines for data collection and sharing
- Cite datasets appropriately in publications

For detailed guidelines, please refer to our [Data Management Guidelines]({{ .Params.guidelines_link }}) and [Data Sharing Policy]({{ .Params.data_policy_link }}).
