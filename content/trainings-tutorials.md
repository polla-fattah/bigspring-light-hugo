## Training Programs

Our training programs are designed for:

- **Graduate Students**: Master's and PhD students developing research skills
- **Early-Career Researchers**: Postdoctoral researchers and junior faculty
- **Senior Researchers**: Experienced researchers seeking to update skills
- **Research Staff**: Technical staff and research assistants

## Past Workshops

We regularly conduct workshops on topics including:
- Research methodology and design
- Statistical analysis and data science
- Research ethics and integrity
- Grant writing and funding opportunities
- Scientific writing and publication
- Data management and open science
- Research communication and outreach
