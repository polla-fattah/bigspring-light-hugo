## Overview

The Environmental Studies Unit conducts research on environmental sustainability, climate change, and ecological conservation. Our research addresses critical environmental challenges facing the Kurdistan Region.

## Research Areas

- Climate change impacts
- Environmental sustainability
- Ecological conservation
- Environmental monitoring
- Natural resource management

## Key Projects

Our unit leads projects on Climate Change Impact Assessment and Environmental Monitoring Systems.

## Facilities

- Environmental monitoring equipment
- Laboratory facilities
- Field research capabilities
- Data collection systems
