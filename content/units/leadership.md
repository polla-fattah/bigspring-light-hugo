## Overview

The Leadership unit represents the research center's administration and strategic direction. This includes the General Director, President, and administrative staff.

## Structure

- General Director
- President of the University
- Administrative staff
- Support personnel

## Responsibilities

- Strategic planning
- Research coordination
- Administrative oversight
- Policy development
