SURC

The Salahaddin University Research Center (SURC) serves as a multidisciplinary hub for advancing knowledge, innovation, and evidence-based inquiry within the Kurdistan Region. As part of one of the oldest and most reputable universities in Iraq, SURC is dedicated to transforming academic research into impactful contributions for both scientific communities and society at large. The center provides an enabling environment for postgraduate researchers, early-career scholars, and senior academics to collaborate on cutting-edge studies that bridge theory, practice, and community needs.

Vision

To be a regional and international leader in interdisciplinary research, fostering an environment where intellectual creativity, scientific rigor, and societal responsibility converge to address local and global challenges.

Mission

Our mission is to advance knowledge through impactful, ethical, and interdisciplinary research that addresses both scientific challenges and societal needs. SURC is committed to fostering collaboration, supporting innovation, and ensuring that research outcomes translate into real-world benefits.

Core Pillars

Bridging Knowledge and Society

Connecting laboratory research with community challenges to generate evidence-based solutions.

Strengthening Research Capacity

Providing training, mentorship, and resources to empower emerging and established researchers.

Promoting Ethics and Excellence

Upholding professional standards, research integrity, and transparent dissemination of findings.

Enabling Innovation and Collaboration

Encouraging creative ideas, securing grants, and building strong partnerships locally and globally.

Strategic Developmental Goals

Leadership and Pioneering

Establish SURC as a recognized leader in the research landscape of Kurdistan and beyond by fostering innovation, setting benchmarks for excellence, and cultivating a pioneering research culture.

Enhancing Research Capacity and Performance

Build competencies of researchers through training programs, workshops, and methodological support. Capacity development will prioritize young scholars while leveraging the experience of senior academics.

Support and Execution of Vital Research

Provide systematic support for impactful projects—from grant writing to publication—ensuring that innovative and socially relevant research is executed effectively and disseminated widely.

Stakeholder Engagement

Strengthen partnerships with government bodies, industry, NGOs, and international organizations to ensure that research outcomes translate into policies, services, and innovations with real-world impact.

Visibility and Publicity of Research Outcomes

Promote the dissemination of research findings through media, conferences, social platforms, and academic networks, ensuring research does not remain confined to academic shelves but contributes actively to public discourse.

Sustainability and Ethical Standards

Safeguard the credibility of research by enforcing codes of conduct, intellectual property rights, and ethical compliance. Sustainability will be secured through institutional branding, long-term funding strategies, and global recognition.
